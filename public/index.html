Welcome to the Odin Super League

We are the premier basketball super league here in Utah. We have been around for over 30 years and we strive to make the game of basketball as competitive and fun as possible. Our league offers kids K-12 the best opportunity possible to get exposure to the more competitive side of basketball. Our super league attracts eyes from top teams all around the nation who are looking to recruit young talent to play for them.

Tryouts for the All-Star team begin on June 6th at 10AM. Only players who were selected by their coaches may try out.

The gyms that we use to host our games are of premium quality and allow for very comfy seating for those coming to watch the games. The referees we hire are highly trained professionals in the game of basketball and help facilitate games as best as possible.

Teams need to register in advance in order to be placed on the league's roster. Our league tends to fill up very quickly so be sure to have all of your team's info ready when you are about to register. For more information on how to register your team and the policies we have please call one of our amazing staff at 888-888-888.


At the end of the season there will be a playoff tournament. There will be 3 total rounds and teams will play a 3 game series. The top 6 teams with the best record at the end of the season will be allowed to play in the tournament. There will not be a losers bracket, as soon as a team is eliminated then they are done for the season.
